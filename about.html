WELCOME TO HAPPY MIND

Happy Mind is Bangladesh's first and largest blended model of mental health care, leading in mental health innovation. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, compassionate, and individualized mental health care, ensuring everyone in Bangladesh has early, affordable, and stigma-free access to support. Our expert team of top psychiatrists, experienced psychologists, skilled counselors, and dedicated nurses offer a wide range of services including individual therapy, group therapy, family counseling, medication management, and crisis intervention. With a patient-centered approach, innovative treatments, and a supportive environment, we are committed to excellence in mental health care.
